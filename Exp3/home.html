Welcome to ACE CAR Company

We thank you for visiting our website. ACE CAR Company is one of the leading names in Automobiles dealership of Maruti Suzuki. ACE Car Company Pvt. Ltd. a company incorporated under the Companies Act having its registered office at Kanwal Complex, A G Office Road, Gwalior came into existence as a Private Limited Company on 28th November, 1990. Mr. Charanjeet Nagpal is the main promoter of ACE Car Company Pvt. Ltd. who established the organisation in the year 1990 when he acquired a dealership of Rajdoot Motorcycles, formerly a proprietary firm called “Prem Motors”. With the motto of Customer Satisfaction, ACE Car Company started its journey with Maruti Suzuki in Gwalior in the year 2001.ACE is now the 4th largest Dealership Group of Maruti Suzuki in India. ACE Car Company as on date operates through 54 Showrooms, 41 Workshops, 7 True Value Outlets, 6 Commercial Outlets & 3 Driving Schools of Maruti Suzuki. In addition, we also have 1 Showroom along with 1 Workshop of Tata Commercial at Bhopal & Indore. We are spread across Rajasthan, Delhi, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Uttarakhand and Uttar Pradesh. ACE Car Company is a one-stop-shop for Sales, Service, Accessories, Finance and Insurance. We are committed to deliver the best of Maruti Suzuki to you. Because we care for you… Always!!
